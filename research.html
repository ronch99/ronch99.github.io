Research

  • How can computers mimic such generation and social communication like human mathematically? How can computers interact and collaborate with humans like a social being?

Projects

Counts and Measurements in Scientific Texts
While the current literature has been focusing on language modeling with large pretrained architechtures, numeracy in texts has not received enough attention. However, the ability to understand semantics of numbers with various numerations is critical for certain NLP tasks, especially those closely connected with scientific and medical texts.

In this project, we are investigating the newly released dataset on counts and measurements at SemEval 2021 Task 8. We plan to conduct data analysis for frequent features and patterns within the dataset and build deep learning models to solve the proposed recognition task.
