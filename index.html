Research

Conversation is one of the most fundamental language usages and conveys various knowledge and information. By studying human conversations as data, I want to build better Conversational AI that effectively extracts and grounds social and professional knowledge to interact with humans:

  • Extracting knowledge from conversations:
    summarize informative conversational texts and learn social factors from conversations to make AI more human-like and user-friendly
  • Grounding knowledge in conversations:
    retrieve structured or unstructured knowledge in texts to generate contextualized dialogue responses and collaborate with humans
My current research is focusing on:
  • Semantic parsing: parsing human questions to SQL queries and retreive structured knowledge from databases
  • Dialogue systems: building robust dialogue systems for different ends (task-oriented bot, supportive chatbot, etc.)
