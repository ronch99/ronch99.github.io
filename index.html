Research

Conversation is one of the most fundamental language usages and involves various knowledge (social, professional, commonsense, etc). By playing with language data, I am interested in building better Conversational AI that can collaborate with human, especially Document-grounded Dialogue and Conversational Question Answering.

My current research is focusing on:
  • Semantic parsing: parsing human questions to SQL queries and retreive structured knowledge from databases
  • Dialogue systems: building robust dialogue systems for different ends (task-oriented bot, supportive chatbot, etc.)
